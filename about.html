About Movad

Helping You Move Toward Your Next Adventure

‘mov-’ - a Latin prefix of the word ‘movere’, translating to “move”

‘ad-’ - a Latin prefix translating to “toward”

At Movad, we are dedicated to helping you move toward your dream travel experiences. We believe that travel is more than just a journey; it's a movement towards self-discovery, adventure, and new horizons.

Our Mission

At Movad, our mission is to inspire you to experience the world in all its richness. We believe that travel is not just about reaching a destination but about immersing yourself in diverse cultures, meeting new people, and savoring unique cuisines. It's about stepping out of your comfort zone and embracing the challenges and joys that come with exploring unfamiliar places.

Through personalized itineraries and expert guidance, Movad is dedicated to helping you discover and connect with the world on a deeper level. Let us help you move toward new adventures, where every journey is an opportunity to grow, learn, and truly experience the vibrant tapestry of our planet.
